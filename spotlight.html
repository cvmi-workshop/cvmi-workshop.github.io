Call for Work-in-Progress Spotlight

We are happy to allocate time for the Work-in-Progress Spotlight session, designed to give researchers a platform to showcase their near-completed works to the community for feedback, giving them exposure, as well as the community a preview of their upcoming projects.

To be considered for this session, please send a 1-page abstract of the work-in-progress by no later than May 11, 2021 to Mei Chen.
