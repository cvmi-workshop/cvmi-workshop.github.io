Welcome

The 11th IEEE Workshop on Computer Vision for Multimodal Microscopy Image Analysis (CVMI) will be held in conjunction with the CVPR 2026 conference.

The emphasis of the 2026 CVMI workshop will be leveraging the advances in large foundational models (LFMs) to improve the multimodal analysis of microscopy and omics data with enhanced explainability. We aim to promote discussions on methods that offer explainability, interpretability, and usefulness for disease classification, prediction, and treatment, while integrating domain knowledge with LFMs.

This workshop will bring together computer vision experts from academia, industry, and government who have made progress in developing computer vision tools for multimodal microscopy image analysis. It will provide a comprehensive forum on this topic and foster in-depth discussion of technical and application issues as well as cross-displinary collaboration. It will also serve as an introduction to researchers and students curious about this important and fertile field.
